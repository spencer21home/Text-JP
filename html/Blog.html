Welcome to Our Juicy Penny Juice Website

Posted on June 20th 2020 by Lily

Producers make juice that is “not from concentrate” by juicing the fruit before pasteurizing it. Pasteurizing involves heating the juice to kill pathogens. Manufacturers then package the juice and sell it. Juice that manufacturers describe as “from concentrate” has one extra step in its manufacturing process. Firstly, the producers juice the fruit. They then filter it in a process that extracts the water from the juice. This means the juice takes up less space during transportation. Before pasteurizing, makers add water back to the juice concentrate. After pasteurizing, manufacturers then package the juice for sale.
